    ✔ The main problem statement of this project is...

    Lack of medical personnel

    ✔ What is medical personnel?

    Medical personnel are healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and others, providing diagnosis, treatment, and health management services.

    ✔ Causes lack of medical personnel

    → Physician burnout

    → Low wages and poor working conditions

    → High salaries and benefits abroad

    ✔ Effect of lack fo medical personnel

    → Delayed Treatment

    → Overburdened Staff

    → Economic Impact

    → Limited Access to Care
